# Problem 18.27

## Question
(I) What is the maximum power consumption of a 3.0-V port- able CD player that draws a maximum of 240 mA of current?

## Final answer from source
0.72 W.

## Worked solution
Use the electric power relation:

\[
P = IV
\]

where  
- \(P\) is power,  
- \(I\) is current,  
- \(V\) is voltage.

Given:
\[
V = 3.0\ \text{V}
\]
\[
I = 240\ \text{mA} = 0.240\ \text{A}
\]

Substitute into the formula:

\[
P = (0.240\ \text{A})(3.0\ \text{V}) = 0.720\ \text{W}
\]

Rounding appropriately:

\[
P = 0.72\ \text{W}
\]

## Key concepts used
- Electric power relation: \(P = IV\)
- Unit conversion: \(240\ \text{mA} = 0.240\ \text{A}\)
- Multiplication of voltage and current to find power
