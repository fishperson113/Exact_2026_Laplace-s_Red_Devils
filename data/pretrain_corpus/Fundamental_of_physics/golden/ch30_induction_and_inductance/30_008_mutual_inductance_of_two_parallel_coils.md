# Sample Problem 30.08: Mutual inductance of two parallel coils

## Problem
Sample Problem 30.08  
Mutual inductance of two parallel coils

Figure 30-20 shows a small coil located at the center of a large coil. The mutual inductance of the coils can be determined by sending current $i_1 $ through the large coil.

## Key ideas
Mutual induction: if a changing current in one coil induces an emf in another, the relation is
$$ 
\mathcal{E}_2 = -M \frac{di_1}{dt}, \qquad \mathcal{E}_1 = -M \frac{di_2}{dt},
 $$
where $M $ is the mutual inductance.
