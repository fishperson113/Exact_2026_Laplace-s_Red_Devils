# Problem 18.37

## Question
(II) How many kWh of energy does a 550-W toaster use in the morning if it is in operation for a total of 5.0 min? At a cost of estimate how much this would add to your monthly electric energy bill if you made toast four mornings per week. 9.0 cents/kWh,

## Final answer from source
0.046 kWh; 6.6 cents per month.

## Worked solution
Use the relation between power, energy, and time:

$$ 
E = Pt
 $$

### 1) Convert the toaster power to kilowatts
$$ 
550\ \text{W} = 0.550\ \text{kW}
 $$

### 2) Convert the operating time to hours
$$ 
5.0\ \text{min} = \frac{5.0}{60}\ \text{h} = 0.0833\ \text{h}
 $$

### 3) Compute the energy used
$$ 
E = (0.550\ \text{kW})(0.0833\ \text{h}) = 0.0458\ \text{kWh}
 $$

Rounding appropriately:

$$ 
E \approx 0.046\ \text{kWh}
 $$

### 4) Find the cost per morning
Electricity costs 9.0 cents per kWh, so:

$$ 
\text{cost per morning} = 0.0458\ \text{kWh}\times 9.0\ \frac{\text{cents}}{\text{kWh}}
= 0.412\ \text{cents}
 $$

### 5) Estimate monthly cost for 4 mornings per week
Take a month as about 4 weeks:

$$ 
4\ \text{mornings/week}\times 4\ \text{weeks/month} = 16\ \text{mornings/month}
 $$

Then:

$$ 
16 \times 0.412\ \text{cents} = 6.59\ \text{cents}
 $$

Rounded:

$$ 
\boxed{6.6\ \text{cents per month}}
 $$

So the toaster uses:

$$ 
\boxed{0.046\ \text{kWh}}
 $$

and adds about:

$$ 
\boxed{6.6\ \text{cents per month}}
 $$

to the electric bill.

## Key concepts used
- Electrical energy relation: $E = Pt $
- Unit conversion: watts to kilowatts
- Unit conversion: minutes to hours
- Cost of electricity = energy used × rate
- Monthly estimate using 4 weeks per month
